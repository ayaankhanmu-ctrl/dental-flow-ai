Questions We Hear Often

Straight answers, no fluff

How quickly will we see results? +

Most practices recover their first missed-call bookings within 24 hours of going live. Reactivation campaigns typically see responses within the first 48 hours. We're usually set up and running within two working days of you signing on.

Does my reception team need to do anything differently? +

No. That's the point. Dental Flow works in the background — handling conversations, answering questions, and booking appointments. Your team sees the bookings appear in the diary. They don't manage the process that created them.

Will patients know they're talking to an automated system? +

We write every message to sound like it came from your practice — using your name, your tone, and the patient's own history with you. Patients regularly reply warmly, often commenting on how attentive the communication feels. You control and can edit every template.

What practice management software do you work with? +

We integrate with the most widely used UK practice management systems including Dentally, Software of Excellence, Exact, and R4. If yours is less common, we'll check compatibility during your consultation at no charge — and we've yet to encounter a practice we couldn't work with.

What if a patient replies with a complaint or negative message? +

Any conversation that indicates a complaint or negative experience is flagged immediately and escalated to your team — it never stays in the automated flow. For review requests, patients who seem less than satisfied are routed to a private feedback form rather than your Google page, protecting your rating.

Can Dental Flow handle Invisalign and treatment enquiries? +

Yes. We build structured text conversations specifically for high-value treatments — walking prospects through crowding assessments, cost questions, finance options, and treatment timelines. When they're ready, they're handed straight to your treatment coordinator as a warm, informed lead.

Is there a long-term contract? +

No lock-in. We run on monthly rolling terms. No annual contracts, no cancellation penalties. We'd rather keep your business by being indispensable than by making it difficult to leave.

Is patient data handled securely? +

All data is processed in accordance with UK GDPR. We never share or sell patient information. Our messaging infrastructure is encrypted end-to-end, and we can provide a full data processing agreement (DPA) on request.
