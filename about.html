Our Story

Built from frustration. Refined by results.

Our founders came from backgrounds in digital marketing and dental practice management. After working with dozens of practices, they kept seeing the same problem: brilliant clinicians, hardworking reception teams, and still — chairs sitting empty.

Patients were slipping through the cracks. Missed calls went unanswered. Consultation leads received one follow-up email and were never heard from again. High-value Invisalign enquiries were lost because nobody had time to nurture them.

Dental Flow was built to solve exactly these problems — using automation and AI to do the patient communication work that your team doesn't have time for, at a standard your team would be proud of.

Our mission: help every dental practice operate at its true revenue potential — without burning out their team.

2022

Founded in London

Two co-founders, one shared frustration, and a determination to fix patient communication for good.

2023

First 50 practices onboarded

Refined our core four services based on real-world results and direct feedback from practice owners.

Introduced intelligent two-way SMS — enabling structured Invisalign and high-value treatment conversations at scale.

Serving practices across the UK

Helping dental practices recover an average of £18,000 in additional monthly revenue on complete autopilot.
